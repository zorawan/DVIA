Colors of Post Impressionism in The Metropolitan Museum of Art

What colors did post impressionism artists used for their masterpices?

We can analize colors from each paintings and in order to find a regular pattern of artists' color preference. We can see the connection between colors and the objects that artists painted and maybe learn to mimic the sence and the feeling of post impressionism.
