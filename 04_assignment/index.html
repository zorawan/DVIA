This Map Is About Earthquakes 2021
Facts

The earthquakes with a magnitude smaller than 3 often occur in Alaska and some areas in Northern America. 3 to 4 magnitude occur from the north pacific ocean to the west coast of America, and 4 to 4.5 magnitude occur around the pacific rim. Some of them also happen on the Eurasian Plate. The earthquakes larger than 4.5 magnitudes often occur around the pacific rim and the Indian Plate.
